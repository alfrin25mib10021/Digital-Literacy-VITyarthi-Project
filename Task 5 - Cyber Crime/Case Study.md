# 🪱UPI Fraud🪱 #

UPI fraud is one of the most common types of online scams in India today, especially among students and those who are new to online payment systems. UPI fraud generally occurs when a person is tricked into sending money.

For instance, the person is called by the scammer, who claims to be the bank employee or the customer service representative or the buyer/seller of the person. He/she tells the person, “You are going to receive the money,” and then sends the person a request to pay the money. However, the person thinks that they need to accept the payment request to receive the money. But the truth is, the person is sending their money.

Another type of UPI fraud is when the person is tricked into sending their money through fake links that are created to look like the original bank website. When the person enters their UPI PIN or the OTP, the scammer gets access to their account.

This is another reason how important it is to be vigilant and know the real process of how online payment systems work before using them
