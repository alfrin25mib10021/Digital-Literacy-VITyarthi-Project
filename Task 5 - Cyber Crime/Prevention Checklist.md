# Prevention Checklist General Safety #
Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone . 
Don’t click on unknown or suspicious links .
Always double-check who is calling before trusting them . 
Use strong passwords and don’t reuse them . 
Turn on two-factor authentication .
Check website links carefully before entering details

# UPI Safety #
You don't need to enter pin to receive money 
Never accept unknown payment request

# Reporting #
Report fraud @ : cybercrime.gov.in
Call 1930 immediately if money is lost
